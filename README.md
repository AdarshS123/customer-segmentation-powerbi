# 📊 Customer Segmentation & Revenue Intelligence Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🔍 Overview
This project presents a data-driven customer segmentation dashboard built using Power BI. It analyses customer behaviour, revenue performance, and profitability to identify key business insights and retention opportunities.

---

## 🚀 Features
- Customer segmentation (High Value, At Risk, Mid Value, Low Engagement)
- Revenue, Profit, and AOV analysis
- Monthly revenue trends and seasonality insights
- Geographic revenue distribution across Australian states
- Top customer analysis
- Interactive filters (state, service category)

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- ~66% of customers are at risk, highlighting retention challenges
- Mentoring Programs generate the highest revenue and profit
- Revenue is concentrated among a small group of high-value customers
- NSW and QLD contribute the highest regional revenue
- Revenue shows seasonal fluctuations across months

---

## 🎯 Recommendations
- Implement targeted retention campaigns for at-risk customers
- Focus marketing on high-performing services
- Expand in high-performing regions and improve low-performing areas
- Introduce upselling and cross-selling strategies
- Stabilise revenue with consistent monthly campaigns

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualisation)
- Power Query (Data Cleaning)
- DAX (Measures & KPIs)
- Excel Dataset
